## Projects

### 🏦 SusuPay *(Month 1 — In Progress)*
Digital manager for rotating savings groups (Susu/Tontine/Njangi).
30 million West Africans manage savings informally — this digitises it.
`React` `Context API` `LocalStorage`

### 🌱 AgroChain *(Month 2)*
Farmer-to-buyer marketplace eliminating middlemen in agricultural 
supply chains. Farmers earn more. Buyers pay less.
`React` `Node.js` `Express` `MongoDB`

### 🏥 MediFind *(Month 3 — Capstone)*
Connects patients to nearest clinics, pharmacies and health workers.
Built for low-bandwidth environments across West Africa.
`Full MERN Stack` `Geolocation` `Low-bandwidth optimised`
